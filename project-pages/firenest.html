FireNest

In January 2024, a group of us entered the Climate Solutions Hackathon hosted by the Wilkes Center for Climate Science and Policy. We had 24 hours to develop a solution for wildfires, focusing on prediction, risk mitigation, alert systems, community resilience, or health hazards, and present it in a slide deck.

We won 2nd place with our concept for FireNest, a development company aimed at creating fire-safe communities committed to environmental sustainability. We later presented at the 2024 Wilkes Climate Summit with the poster on the right, where we took 1st place in the Undergraduate Poster Contest.

My role was to design the practical aspects of these fire-safe communities, addressing the vulnerabilities of current homes to wildfires.
