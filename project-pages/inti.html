INTI Coffee

INTI Specialty Coffee is a family-owned business dedicated to bringing premium, single-origin Peruvian coffee to coffee lovers in a sustainable and ethical way. Founded in Cusco, Peru, the company specializes in direct trade and organic practices, establishing close relationships with local farmers to ensure that each coffee bean is responsibly sourced, shade-grown, and hand-picked.

Duration

2 weeks

Role

Web Designer

Tools

Discover

I recently introduced a friend to INTI Coffee, a local café near my university. When they asked for the café's website to check the menu, I provided the link, even though I hadn't visited the site myself. After looking at it, my friend remarked on the challenging navigation, pointing out that the navigation bar didn't align with their expectations for a website. They also noted that the information felt scattered across multiple pages, making it difficult to find what they were looking for.

Project Overview

  • Navigation and Layout Issues

    • The website's navigation layout is cluttered, containing 8 elements and breaking the Rule of 4.5

  • Inconsistent Visual and Design Elements

    • Inconsistencies in fonts, colors, and formatting disrupt the website's visual cohesion and readability

  • Content and Information Presentation

    • Important information is either missing or poorly presented

  • Redundant or Confusing Functionalities

    • Repeated content across various pages that at first glance may appear to be different create confusion

  • Incorrect of Missing Information

    • Key information is incorrect or incomplete, leading to potential frustration

Design Solution

User Journey

Competitive Analysis

We analyzed the features that various SWE websites contained across colleges across the country. From this analysis, we realize that SWE at the University of Utah has a higher need to make event information accessible as they often plan and host events within a shorter timeframe than SWE at other campuses.

Furthermore, SWE at the U heavily wants to emphasize ways to get involved with the organization, which makes the accessibility of contact, event, and sponsorship information high priority.
