FireNest

Introduction

In January of 2024, I and a few others formed a group to enter the annual Climate Solutions Hackathon hosted by the Wilkes Center for Climate Science and Policy. Within 24 hours, we were to design a solution to wildfires that focused on prediction, risk mitigation, alert systems, community resilience or health hazards and lay out our idea onto a slide deck.

Through this process, we won 2nd place by creating a development company called FireNest that focuses on designing and developing fire-safe communities with a strong commitment to environmental sustainability. Furthermore, we presented our idea to the 2024 Wilkes Climate Summit, winning 1st place in the Undergraduate Poster Contest.

My role on the team was to design what these fire-safe communities would look like in practice, keeping in mind all the dangers that current homes have against wildfires.
