A Little About Me

I'm Sarah Choe, a student at the University of Utah, where I'm diving into a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science along with a minor in Design and Psychology. I’m really passionate about blending tech and design to create user experiences that are not only functional but also bring people together in meaningful ways.

When I'm not buried in code or design projects, you can usually find me at a café sketching ideas or working on personal projects. I love taking my dog for long walks, which helps me clear my mind and find inspiration in the everyday. If nothing else, you might find me watching one too many thriller TV shows. Overall, I'm all about combining creativity and technology to make life a little easier and more enjoyable for everyone.
