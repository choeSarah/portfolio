Sonder

Introduction

Sonder is a case study that delves into the intricacies of the design process, focusing on both broad design principles and the nuances of my personal approch to design.

The primary aim of this project was to redesign an enhancement to a space that serves as a sanctuary for individuals seeking solace and inspiration. The environment was envisioned as a haven where one could engage in reflection, meditation, and nurture a sense of hope. Througout the project, I identified and documented various aspects that contributed to the essence of the space, which involved writing, photography, diagramming, and drawing.

The design process of Sonder culminated in a re-imagining of what Barnes and Nobles could be that not only met functional requirements, but also embodied the qualities of reflection, meditation, and hope. The project highlighted the significance of being in a public space with many strangers, all people whose lives and stories we are not aware about.
