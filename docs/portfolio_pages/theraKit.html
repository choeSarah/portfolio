QuestMind

Introduction

QuestMind is a mental health app that complements traditional therapy with personalized features. The goal is to empower users to manage their mental health effectively between therapy sessions.

This page consists of the workflow of the project, from problem definition to UX research to prototyping.

Duration

10

Weeks

Tools

Problem Definition

Painpoint Overview

  1. Although many therapists assign users "homework" or self-help assignments to complete between sessions, many users struggle to maintain motivation.
  2. This lack of motivation can hinder one's progress in their mental health and prevent them from fully benefiting from therapy.

Design Challenge

How might we design a mental wellness app that complements therapy with structured, engaging, and motivational support to fill gaps in mental health services?

User Persona

Emily Lee

Individual

About

Age: 19 years old
Location: SLC, Utah
Hobbies: Playing video games and watching movies

Situation

Emily is a college student receiving therapy through her campus student services.

Challenge

Despite efforts to assign homework between sessions, Emily struggles with motivation and tracking her progress.

Need

Emily needs a way to stay motivated and organized to complete her therapy homework effectively.

Rebecca Jorgensen

Therapist

About

Age: 35 years old
Location: SLC, Utah
Hobbies: Camping and hiking

Situation

Rebecca works primarily with younger clients and finds cognitive behavioral therapy effective. However, her clients often struggle with homework assignments between sessions.

Challenge

Clients face difficulties understanding assignments, recognizing their benefits, and overcoming the mental barriers associated with task completion.

Need

Rebecca needs a way to keep her clients motivated and engaged in completing their therapy homework.

Interviews

I interviewed three clinical psychology professors over video call to understand the challenges many therapists and clients face in being motivated to finish their homework. A few sample interview questions are listed as below:

  1. What do you see as the biggest barriers to clients completing therapy homework?
  2. How do you think technology can enhance traditional therapy practices?
  3. What are some common factors that influence a client’s motivation to complete therapy homework?
  4. What features do you believe are essential in an app designed to support clients between therapy sessions?
  5. What are the key challenges clients face when using digital tools for mental health support?

Qualitative Research

After gaining the key insights from the interviews, I conducted basic ethnographic research by diving into Reddit, Twitter, and Facebook forums regarding CBT to understand clients better.
